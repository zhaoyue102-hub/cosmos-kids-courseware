- TRAPPIST-1：NASA Exoplanet Exploration，七颗地球大小的岩石行星围绕一颗超冷红矮星运行。https://science.nasa.gov/universe/exoplanets/discovery-alert-a-record-7-earth-sized-planets-orbiting-a-single-star/
